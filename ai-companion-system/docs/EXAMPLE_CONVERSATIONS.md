## Tips for Great Search Conversations

1. **Be specific** about what you want to know
2. **Ask follow-ups** to dive deeper into topics
3. **Share your perspective** and let the character respond
4. **Use natural language** - talk like you would to a friend
5. **Combine with character personality** - they'll respond in their unique style

## Search Triggers Reference

Messages that trigger automatic searches:

- "What is...", "What are..."
- "Who is...", "Who are..."
- "When did...", "When was..."
- "Tell me about..."
- "Latest", "Recent", "Current"
- "News", "Breaking", "Headlines"
- "What's happening", "Update on"
- "This week", "Today", "Now"
- "Look up", "Search for", "Find out"
